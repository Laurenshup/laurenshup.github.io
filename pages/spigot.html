Spigot

This is my Spigot page. Spigot is the more expanded version of Minecraft. Minecraft has 2 play styles: Single Player and Multi Player. In Multi Player you can join a 'server'. A server runs on a JAR (Program). The default JAR is Minecraft's server JAR. But you can't do anything with it. That's why there are modifications of that JAR. That allows Developers to make 'plugins'. Plugins are JARs that you can run on those modificated JARs that allow you to make Minigames. There are many modificated JARs with their own API. The most used JARs (that are still updated) are Spigot, CraftBukkit and Sponge. They all have their own API, so you can't make one JAR for all of the server types. That means that you can only make plugins for one type. But Spigot and CraftBukkit are the same. They both use Bukkit as main program (Bukkit is not updated anymore). But Spigot is more configurable than CraftBukkit, that means that Spigot has less lag than CraftBukkit.

I have a lot of plugins. I don't have all plugins on Spigot. But the ones that are on Spigot, they are amazing. You should try them out! They add a lot of functionality. Recently I published a new plugin: Infection. It's paid, but it is a whole minigame in one plugin. I have a total of 7 published plugins on Spigot. I have more Spigot plugins but not all of them are on Spigot the site. You can check my published Plugins here below or on my Spigot profile page.
